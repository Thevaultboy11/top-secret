  <p class="blog-tracing">

<div class="quote blog-tracing">
  <h2 class="blog-tracing">Get your hands dirty</h2>
  <p class="blog-tracing">
    When it comes to mastering penetration testing and cybersecurity, gaining hands-on experience is an essential
    component of the learning process. Learning by doing allows you to apply your knowledge and skills in practical
    scenarios.
  </p>
  <p class="blog-tracing">
    To help you get started, we have curated a list of resources that offer opportunities for hands-on experience in
    hacking.
  </p>
  <ul class="blog-tracing">
    <li class="blog-tracing">
      <strong>TryHackMe</strong> - A fantastic platform for beginners, providing a variety of interesting rooms and
      challenges to learn from. - <a href="https://tryhackme.com/">Link</a>
    </li>
    <li class="blog-tracing">
      <strong>OverTheWire</strong> - Offers fun and bite-sized CTF-like challenges that help you learn Linux commands
      while practicing hacking and privilege escalation. - <a href="https://overthewire.org/wargames/">Link</a>
    </li>
    <li class="blog-tracing">
      <strong>HackTheBox</strong> - A platform tailored to the needs of more experienced hackers. -{' '}
      <a href="https://hackthebox.com/">Link</a>
    </li>
    <li class="blog-tracing">
      <strong>Portswigger</strong> - The go-to platform for learning web application vulnerabilities, offering a
      real-world application with actual vulnerabilities to explore. -{' '}
      <a href="https://portswigger.net/web-security/">Link</a>
    </li>
  </ul>
